<section id="about">


<h2>About</h2>


<p>


The Social Fabric of Care is a research initiative exploring how care sustains social life.


Care is often treated as a specific form of work or a professional activity. This initiative approaches care differently: as a social fabric that connects institutions, professions, families, and communities.


</p>


<p>


The project examines how care is organized across welfare systems, professional settings, civil society organizations, and everyday life.


</p>


<p>


Led by <strong>Dr. Inbar Livnat</strong>, the research focuses on care work, welfare professions, gender, and the boundaries between work, home, and social life.


</p>


</section>
